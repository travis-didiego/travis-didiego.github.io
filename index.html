Who am I?

I am a youth development soccer coach, making a transition into the world of Inbound Marketing. Over the course of the last 7 years, I built my reputation (brand) as a coach. As I became more and more established and my client base grew, my mission shifted towards helping other soccer programs grow. That’s when I became an integral part of Sporting Club Premier FC (www.scpfc.com) and began focusing on creating the best soccer brand in the area. From organizing tournaments and training programs, to posting and engaging with customers via social media, I paid attention to the simple cost effective details that would help us grow.
